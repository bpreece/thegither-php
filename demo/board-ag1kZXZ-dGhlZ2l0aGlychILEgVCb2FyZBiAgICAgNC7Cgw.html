PyBake 2013 Rideshare is a service provided by the organizers of the 2013 PyBake Decamp in Minneapolis for the benefit of attendees. The organizers understand that people may want to share cars to cut down on traffic and parking, however they cannot assure your safety. Please be careful: o Ride with people you know if possible. o If you ride with a stranger, get references from their company or from common friends and check them out. o Let your friends and family know who you are riding with, when you will leave, and when you expect to be back. Check in with them when you arrive. Please do not abuse this board. If the adminstrators believe you are abusing it, they will remove your post.
